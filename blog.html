Important FAQ

What is the Noakhali Flood Relief Fund?

The Noakhali Flood Relief Fund is an initiative to provide immediate aid to those affected by the severe flooding in Noakhali, Bangladesh. The funds will be used to distribute food, clean drinking water, medical supplies, and temporary shelter to the victims. Your donation will help restore hope and rebuild the lives of thousands of families who are in urgent need of assistance.

How can I donate to the Feni Flood Relief Fund?

You can donate to the Feni Flood Relief Fund through our secure online platform. Simply select the Feni Flood Relief option, enter the amount you'd like to donate, and choose your payment method. Every contribution, no matter how small, helps those in dire need of support in Feni.

What will the Quota Movement Fund Raising be used for?

The Quota Movement Fund Raising is aimed at supporting the ongoing efforts to advocate for fairer recruitment practices in public sector jobs in Bangladesh. The funds will go towards organizing peaceful protests, raising awareness, and supporting the legal costs of individuals involved in the movement. Your support will help ensure a more equitable future for all citizens.

Is my donation to these funds tax-deductible?

Yes, your donations to the Noakhali Flood Relief Fund, Feni Flood Relief Fund, and Quota Movement Fund Raising are eligible for tax deduction, depending on the regulations in your country. Please consult with your tax advisor for more detailed information. A receipt will be provided for all donations.

Can I donate in kind or only financially?

While financial donations are the most effective way to provide immediate assistance, we also welcome donations in kind, such as food, clothing, medical supplies, or other essential items. Please contact us directly for further instructions on how to donate goods or services to these specific causes.
